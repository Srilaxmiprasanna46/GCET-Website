Mechanical Engineering

The department of Mechanical Engineering was established in the year 2011 with an intake of 60 students. Currently the department offers an under graduate program B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering with an annual intake of 30 students. The department is headed by Dr. R. Sudarshan, He has 24 years of teaching experience in various reputed institutions. There are 3 Professors, 3 Associate Professor and 14 Assistant Professor in the department.
